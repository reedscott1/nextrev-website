Member Link

Membership Management for Clover POS

Overview

Member Link is a comprehensive membership management solution that seamlessly integrates with Clover POS systems. Designed for businesses that run membership programs, Member Link automates tracking, renewals, and member engagement.

Key Features

  • Member Management: Easily add, update, and track member information
  • Clover Integration: Automatic synchronization with your Clover POS system
  • Automatic Renewals: Automated membership renewal processing
  • Android App: Barcode scanning and member lookup on Clover devices
  • Web Dashboard: Comprehensive member management portal
  • Secure & Reliable: Enterprise-grade security and data protection

How It Works

  1. Connect MemberLink to your Clover account
  2. Add members through the web dashboard or import existing data
  3. Members are automatically synced with your Clover POS
  4. Use the Android app to scan member cards at checkout
  5. Track membership status and automate renewals

Availability

Member Link is available through the Clover App Market for merchants using Clover POS systems.
