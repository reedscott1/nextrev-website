Our Team

Leadership driving innovation

Samuel Reed

Chairman of the Board

Founded Next Rev Distribution, Inc. in 2005 and has successfully built and led the company for 20 years as a proven entrepreneur in the technology sector. Under his visionary leadership, Next Rev has established itself as a trusted value-added reseller serving data centers, small business, carrier networks, and high-speed production networks throughout the US and Canada.

Samuel's entrepreneurial success stems from his ability to build long-term client relationships and maintain operational excellence, combined with deep financial management expertise and comprehensive market knowledge. As Chairman of the Board, Samuel oversees major strategic business decisions, monitors quarterly performance reviews, and serves as final decision-maker on significant strategic matters affecting the company's direction and growth.

Brooks Lewis

President & Treasurer

As President and Treasurer, Brooks manages financial and banking relationships and he brings extensive operational expertise to Next Rev. As former Director of Operations for an Amazon DSP, Brooks developed deep knowledge of day to day operations of tactical management strategies.

His professional background includes significant experience at top-tier management consulting firms including Deloitte Consulting, Capco Consulting, and Celerity Consulting, where he led complex projects, managed large teams, and delivered strategic solutions for Fortune 500 clients.

At Next Rev, Brooks focuses on business development, customer relationship management, market intelligence, strategic partnerships, industry networking, and growth strategy.

Scott Reed

Chief Technology Officer

Scott's background provides valuable insight for Next Rev's operations. In his role as Chief Technology Officer he creates and manages technology systems for Next Rev Distribution, Inc. He brings over 15 years of experience as a software developer with deep expertise in creating custom solutions for the transportation industry, most notably through his extensive work at Norfolk Southern, one of the nation's premier freight rail carriers.

Scott's analytical focus includes profitability analysis and KPI reporting, ensuring compliance while maintaining operational efficiency.
