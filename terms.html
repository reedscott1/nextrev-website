2. Services Description

Next Rev develops software for Clover POS devices. Our current products include:

2.1 Member Link

A Clover Android app for merchants with private memberships. Member Link lets staff scan a membership card to open a tab, attach a member to an in-progress order from the Clover payment screen, and keep numeric table names in sync with the member seated at each table.

  • Runs on the Clover device
  • Connects to the Clover REST API to read customer records, read and write orders, and read and write merchant configuration (tables, order types)
  • No account or login required beyond your Clover merchant account
  • Does not transmit data to Next Rev or any third party

2.2 Tip Out Calculator

A calculator for distributing shift tips among employees by category on Clover devices.

  • Runs on the Clover device
  • Connects to the Clover REST API to retrieve employee shift data
  • Reads order, inventory, and employee data from your Clover merchant account (read-only)
  • No account or login required beyond your Clover merchant account
  • Does not transmit data to Next Rev or any third party
