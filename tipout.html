Tip Out Calculator

Fair Tip Distribution by Category for Clover POS

Overview

Tip Out Calculator makes it easy to split tips fairly among staff based on sales by category. Select an employee shift, and the app calculates tip out amounts based on your configured percentages per printer label category.

Key Features

  • Category-Based Tip Outs: Configure tip out percentages for each printer label category (bar, food, etc.)
  • Shift-Aware: Automatically pulls employee shift times to calculate tips for the correct time range
  • Accurate Calculations: Reads actual order and line item data from your Clover device
  • Print Reports: Print tip out breakdowns to your Clover receipt printer
  • Easy Setup: Configure categories and percentages once, then calculate with one tap

How It Works

  1. Install Tip Out Calculator from the Clover App Market
  2. Configure your tip out categories and percentages in Settings
  3. At the end of a shift, open the app - it detects the current employee
  4. Select the shift and view the tip out breakdown by category
  5. Print the report for your records

Requirements

Tip Out Calculator works on Clover Station, Flex, and Flex Pocket devices. Requires printer labels to be configured on your Clover inventory items for category-based calculations.

Availability

Tip Out Calculator is available on the Clover App Market. Contact us with any questions.
