Member Scan

Barcode Scanning for Any Text Field on Clover

Overview

Member Scan connects your Clover device built-in barcode scanner to any text field on screen. Scan member ID cards to look up customers instantly, or use the scanner anywhere you can type.

Key Features

  • Instant Member Lookup: Scan a member ID card to search for customers in the Clover Customers app
  • Works Everywhere: Any focused text field on your device accepts scanned barcode input
  • No Training Needed: Press the scan button like you already do - text appears automatically
  • Keeps Your Keyboard: Runs in the background - your stock keyboard stays exactly as-is
  • No Data Collection: Does not connect to the internet or store any data

How It Works

  1. Install Member Scan from the Clover App Market
  2. Open the app and tap Enable Member Scan
  3. Turn on Member Scan in Accessibility settings
  4. Tap into any text field and scan - the barcode text appears instantly

Requirements

Member Scan works on Clover Station, Flex, and Flex Pocket devices. Member numbers must already be stored in the customer note field for the Customers app lookup to work.

Availability

Member Scan is available on the Clover App Market. Contact us with any questions.
